FUHH.IO — Crafting a crisp brand and product UI

Defined the visual identity, marketing site, and in-product experience for a SaaS tool — so everything from first click to daily use feels like one product.

Problem

The team had strong engineering but no coherent brand. The landing page, dashboard, and onboarding all looked like they came from different products, which eroded trust during evaluation and complicated release storytelling.

Approach

Built a minimal design system grounded in typography and spacing, then extended it across the marketing site, app shell, and email. Worked alongside the founder to tighten the product narrative and reduce onboarding to three essential steps.

Outcome

  • Trial sign-up conversion up 52%
  • Onboarding completion lifted from 34% → 71%
  • Single design system now powers every surface
